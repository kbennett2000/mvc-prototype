MVC Kids serves children from the nursery through 5th grade. Sunday mornings feature age-segmented classes where kids receive biblical instruction before joining their families for worship.

- **Nursery** — Warm, safe care for infants through children who are potty-trained.
- **Wee Worship** — Age-appropriate Bible teaching for preschoolers.
- **Wonder Worship** — Interactive biblical instruction for elementary-aged students.

**Awana** runs Wednesday evenings September through April, organized by age group: Puggles (ages 2–3), Cubbies (3–PreK), Sparks (K–2), and TnT (3rd–5th). Our local club operates with 60+ volunteer leaders, many with decade-long tenure.

Additional offerings include **Story Hour** on Tuesdays at the Children's Museum, featuring topical books and crafts; **Vacation Bible School** in June (June 15–19, 2026 — Emerald Crossing); and a **Children's Christmas Choir** in season.

To get involved or volunteer, contact the church office at admin@mvckiowa.com or 303-491-4339.
