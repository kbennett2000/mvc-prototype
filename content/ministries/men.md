MVC's men's ministry operates two breakfast gatherings designed to foster spiritual growth and fellowship. Both welcome fathers, sons, grandfathers, and grandsons looking for encouragement and brotherhood.

**Elbert County Men of Valor** — Third Saturday of every month at 7:30 AM, American Legion Hall in Elizabeth. Free breakfast. Contact Pastor Brian Janes at 303-243-0167.

**MVC Men's Breakfast** — Last Sunday of every month at 7:00 AM, MVC dining room. Contact Colin Kelly at 720-656-5438.
