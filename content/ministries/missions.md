MVC frames its mission work through Acts 1:8, addressing concentric geographic circles: **local** (Kiowa and Elbert County), **national** (USA), **regional** (Canada, Mexico, Central America), and **global**.

Our theological foundation is the sacrificial love of God — "God so loved the world that He gave" — which we believe calls every congregation to similar generosity. We support both international missionary operations and our own members serving in mission roles. MVC has planted five Colorado churches.

We partner with organizations including **Pioneers** and individually-supported missionary families.
