Overcomers Outreach is a Christ-centered 12-step support network for anyone affected by addiction or compulsive behavior — whether you are personally fighting it or walking through it with a loved one. We serve as a bridge between traditional recovery groups and the church.

We "recover together as we meet to study and grow in God's Word." Our gatherings emphasize:

- Fellowship in recovery
- Reconciliation with God and community
- Education about substances and addiction
- Faith-based edification
- Dedicated service to others

All are welcome, regardless of background.

**Weekly meeting:** Mondays, 6:30–7:30 PM, in the "Large Trailer" on the MVC campus.
