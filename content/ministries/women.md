MVC Women's Ministry offers multiple ways for women to participate — Bible studies, mentoring, discipleship, and monthly Saturday fellowships — built around different schedules and seasons of life.

**Bible Study Fellowship (BSF) Satellite Class** — Studying Romans beginning **September 15, 2026**, meeting Tuesday evenings, 6:00–7:30 PM. Contact Sharon Egan at egancasa@gmail.com or 303-981-9808.

(NOTE: /calendar/ lists BSF on Thursdays — verify before launch.)

**Sewing Class** — Meets biweekly in the church dining hall, 6:00–8:30 PM. Contact Brooke Tartaglia at 623-755-1933.

**Monthly Fellowship** — Second Saturdays at 9:00 AM. The 2025–26 season's final gathering was May 9 (breakfast and a presentation on the Psalms). Schedule for the next season is forthcoming.

Childcare is available for some events. Contact the church office Monday–Thursday at admin@mvckiowa.com or 303-491-4339.
