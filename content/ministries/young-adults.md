The Young Adults ministry is for individuals 18 to about 25 years old — those who have completed high school but are not yet married, navigating a transitional life stage.

We meet **Monday evenings at 6:30 PM in "The Study"** for Bible study, peer accountability, and fellowship. Refreshments are provided.

We're currently working through the **Gospel of Matthew**, exploring how the apostle's account — written by a former tax collector — presents Jesus as the promised Messiah. Matthew's perspective combines Jewish messianic expectations with eyewitness testimony, highlighting Jesus' deity and role as Savior.

To get involved, contact the church office at admin@mvckiowa.com or 303-491-4339.
