MVC Youth serves middle and high school students through two primary programs.

**The View** meets Sunday evenings — high schoolers gather 4:00–5:00 PM and middle schoolers 5:00–6:00 PM — for faith development, relationship building, and biblical learning.

**AWANA** runs Wednesday evenings September through April. **TREK** is for middle schoolers and **Journey** is for high schoolers, both focused on scripture study and memorization. Seniors work toward the Citation Award.

We encourage parental involvement and prioritize honest spiritual growth.

To get involved, contact the church office at admin@mvckiowa.com or 303-491-4339.
