This is a **sample custom page** created to show you how custom pages work.

## What you can do here

- Write any content you like using the formatting toolbar in the admin panel
- Add headings, bold text, bullet lists, links, and images
- Publish the page and it appears immediately on your site at `/pages/sample-page`

## Adding this page to navigation

Once you've created a page, go to **Navigation** in the admin panel and add a new item pointing to `/pages/sample-page` (or whatever slug you chose).

Feel free to delete this page when you're ready to create your own.
