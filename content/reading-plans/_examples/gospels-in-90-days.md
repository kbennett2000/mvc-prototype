A ninety-day journey through all four Gospels — Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John — reading roughly one chapter each day. By the end you will have encountered Jesus through four different lenses: the Jewish Messiah-King, the servant who acts, the friend of the outcast, and the eternal Word made flesh.

The plan uses the SOAP reading method to help you move from reading to reflection to response. Each day takes about 15–20 minutes including journaling.

**Matthew** (days 1–28) traces Jesus as the fulfillment of Israel's long story — every chapter echoes the Old Testament. **Mark** (days 29–44) is the fastest-moving Gospel; notice how often it says "immediately." **Luke** (days 45–68) highlights women, the poor, and outsiders. **John** (days 69–90) circles back to creation and focuses on believing and abiding.
