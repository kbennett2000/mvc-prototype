## About This Plan

The **SOAP method** is a simple framework for engaging scripture each day:

* **S — Scripture:** Read today's psalm slowly and completely.
* **O — Observation:** What does the text say? Note the main ideas, images, or repeated words.
* **A — Application:** What does this mean for your life today? How is God speaking through this passage?
* **P — Prayer:** Respond to God in prayer, guided by what you read.

## May 2026: Psalms 121–150

This plan walks through the final thirty psalms of the Psalter — the "Songs of Ascent" and the great closing doxologies. These psalms were sung by pilgrims on their way to Jerusalem and by the gathered community in worship. They carry themes of trust, protection, God's faithfulness, and full-throated praise.

**Note:** This plan ends May 30 with Psalm 150 — the Psalter's final "Hallelujah." May 31 does not have an assigned reading. See the launch checklist in NOTES.txt for follow-up.
