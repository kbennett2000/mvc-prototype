Join us for this livestream worship service from MajesticViewChurchLive, featuring the message "Victorious Friendship" as part of the "Victorious" series


In this service, Pastor John explores John 15, focusing on the night before Jesus's crucifixion and the vital lessons He shared with His disciples about love, obedience, and relationship
.
Sermon Highlights:
The Source of Love: Discover how God is the ultimate source of agape love and how Jesus models this by abiding in the Father’s love
.
Abide and Obey: Learn why obedience is the natural response to loving God and how abiding in Christ leads to true victory in our relationships with spouses, family, and the church
.
Victorious Joy: Pastor John discusses how true joy is found in fulfilling God’s designed purpose for your life and seeing others find their purpose in Him
.
Sacrificial Friendship: A deep dive into John 15:13, examining what it truly means to "lay down one's life" for a friend by choosing service over self every day
.
Initiation and Choice: Be encouraged to be the one who initiates friendship and to be a "choice" rather than just a "convenience" in the lives of those around you
.

.

.

.

. We are challenged to find an "empty vessel" in our lives and fill it with the love of Jesus
.
