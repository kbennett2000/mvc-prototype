Pastor John’s sermon, "Victory over Hatred," part of the "Victorious" series, explores the inevitability of facing worldly hostility and the biblical path to overcoming it through Christ's love
. Drawing from the Upper Room Discourse in John 15, the message emphasizes that because the world rejected and hated Jesus first, His followers should expect similar treatment
. Pastor John highlights that believers possess a new identity as ambassadors for Christ called to a ministry of reconciliation, which requires replacing the flesh’s "default mode" of anger with a commitment to love even the unlovable
. To achieve this victory, the sermon outlines eight practical application points, including walking in the power of the Holy Spirit, embracing persecution as an opportunity to understand the sufferings of the Savior, and choosing to trust God’s truth when it is hardest to believe
. Ultimately, the message encourages a shift from the legalistic mindset of the law to the transformative power of grace, exemplified by David’s decision to honor King Saul rather than succumb to hatred
