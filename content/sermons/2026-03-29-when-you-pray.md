The sermon titled Victorious, delivered on March 29, 2026, examines John 17 as the true Lord's Prayer because it reveals the heart of Jesus in the upper room before His death
. The message is organized into three sections: Jesus' prayer for Himself and the completion of His mission, His prayer for the security and sanctification of His original disciples, and His prayer for future believers. Central themes include defining eternal life as knowing God, the security of the believer, and a call for Christians to live in unity and love to witness to the world. The service incorporates the practice of praying the scripture, where the Word of God is read back to Him to align the heart with His will. Finally, the sermon invites the congregation to prepare for Holy Week through a twenty-four-hour prayer vigil and the observance of the Lord's Supper.
