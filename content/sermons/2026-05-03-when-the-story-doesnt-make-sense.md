The sermon, titled "The Person the Lord Invites into His Work," explores the biblical narrative of Rahab from Joshua Chapter 2 to illustrate how God chooses unexpected individuals to participate in His divine plan. Pastor John emphasizes that God's grace is available to everyone, regardless of their past, provided they are willing to recognize their sin and its consequences. By highlighting Rahab’s inclusion in the genealogy of Jesus Christ, the message demonstrates that God prioritizes a humble and desperate heart over personal righteousness or social status. Ultimately, the sermon challenges the congregation to humble themselves before the Lord, asserting that such humility is the key to experiencing spiritual revival and being used for His glory.
