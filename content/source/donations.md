# Donations — Giving is our way of offering back to God a portion of what He has blessed us with.

MVC uses Church Center to facilitate online giving. The church emphasizes "cheerful generosity to God's work and the church." End-of-year donation receipts are distributed in January or available real-time through the Church Center App. Contact the church office for additional details.

## Giving methods
- Online via Church Center (accepts ACH and credit/debit cards through Stripe)
- Checks/cash via Sunday offering boxes or office mail slot
- Bank bill-pay (mailed to the church mailing address)
