# Majestic View Church (Welcome to Majestic View Church!)

Majestic View Church invites visitors to join Sunday services at 9 AM or participate in various age-group activities. The church emphasizes community belonging and offers multiple ministries. Members can access the Elevation Community Center for quiet reflection, social gatherings, or children's activities, with space available for community reservations.
