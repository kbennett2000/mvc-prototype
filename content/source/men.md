# Men's Ministry

Majestic View Church operates two men's breakfast ministries designed to foster spiritual growth and fellowship. Both gatherings welcome fathers, sons, grandfathers, and grandsons seeking encouragement and community. Participants enjoy meals, accountability partnerships, and spiritual support in welcoming environments.
