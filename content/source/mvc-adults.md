# MVC Women's Ministry

NOTE: The URL is /mvc-adults/ but the page title and content are entirely about the Women's Ministry. Mapping to prototype slug "women".

Majestic View Church's women's ministry offers multiple ways to participate based on schedules. The program includes Bible studies, mentoring, discipleship opportunities, and monthly Saturday fellowships. The satellite Bible Study Fellowship focuses on scripture study and will transition to studying Romans beginning September 2026, meeting Tuesday evenings. A sewing class meets biweekly in the church dining hall. The monthly second-Saturday fellowships are concluding for the 2025–26 season, with the final gathering on May 9 featuring breakfast and a presentation on the Psalms. Childcare is available for some events.
