# MVC Kids (Kindergarten – 5th grade)

Majestic View Church offers children's programming across multiple weekly programs. Sunday mornings feature age-segmented classes where young learners receive biblical instruction before joining families for worship. The Nursery provides care for infants through potty-trained children. Wee Worship serves preschoolers with age-appropriate Bible teaching; Wonder Worship engages elementary students through interactive instruction.

Awana runs Wednesday evenings September through April, organized by age groups: Puggles (ages 2–3), Cubbies (3–PreK), Sparks (K–2), and TnT (3rd–5th). The local club operates with 60+ volunteer leaders, many with decade-long tenure.

Additional offerings include Story Hour on Tuesdays featuring topical books and crafts, Vacation Bible School in June, and a Children's Christmas Choir. The church actively recruits volunteers across all children's ministries.
