# MVC Youth

Majestic View Church's youth ministry serves middle and high school students through two primary programs. "The View" meets Sunday evenings — high schoolers 4–5 PM, middle schoolers 5–6 PM — for faith development, relationship building, and biblical learning. AWANA runs Wednesday evenings September through April, offering TREK for middle schoolers and Journey for high schoolers, focused on scripture study and memory work. Seniors work toward the Citation Award.
