# On Our Knees for Elbert County

Majestic View Church began a fasting and prayer initiative in January 2023, with church leadership dedicating individual days to seek God's direction for the county. In April 2023, Pastor John extended this invitation to the entire congregation. The ministry emphasizes prayer's transformative power, with reflections from D.L. Moody, Charles Spurgeon, and Billy Graham. The church tracks daily participation through a prayer calendar and documents baptisms and professions of faith since the initiative began as answers to intercession.
