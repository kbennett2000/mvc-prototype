# Our Story — Who We Are

Majestic View Church describes itself as unified in Jesus Christ, committed to obeying God through Scripture with the aid of the Holy Spirit. The congregation emphasizes creating a welcoming community space where visitors find friendship and love. They renamed their facility "Elevation Community Center at Majestic View Church" to reflect this hospitality mission.

The church's theological foundation rests on biblical inspiration, authority, and inerrancy — believing Scripture represents God's actual words, serves as final authority for Christian faith and practice, and contains no errors. These principles inform all aspects of church life.
