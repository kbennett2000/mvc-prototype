# Overcomers Ministry

Overcomers Outreach is a Christ-centered 12-step support network addressing addiction and compulsive behaviors. The ministry welcomes individuals, families, and loved ones affected by addictive behavior, serving as a bridge between traditional recovery groups and churches across denominations. Participants "recover together as we meet to study and grow in God's Word." The program emphasizes fellowship in recovery, reconciliation with God and community, education about substances and addiction, faith-based edification, and dedicated service to others. All are welcome regardless of background.
