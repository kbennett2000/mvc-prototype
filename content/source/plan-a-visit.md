# Plan a Visit — Sunday Service Times?

The church welcomes visitors with a casual atmosphere — "at MVC you'll see it all!" Attendees are encouraged to arrive early to visit the "New Here/Start Here" table for assistance. Visitor parking is available in the front lot facing Highway 86. Fellowship, coffee, and snacks follow service. Kids' and teens' ministries are available, plus adult connection opportunities.
