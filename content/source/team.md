# Team — Elders / Leadership

The page is organized into Elders/Leadership, Staff, Ministry Leaders, and Facility Personnel. No email addresses are listed for individual team members on this page.

## People mentioned

### Elders / Pastors
- John Smith — Elder and Senior Pastor. Received Jesus at age 9, called to preach at 14. Attended Central Baptist College (Arkansas) where he met his wife Debra. Married since 1979, 8 children, 14 grandchildren. Served at multiple Baptist churches before planting Majestic View Church in 1996. Coaches high school basketball.
- Brian Janes — Elder & Men's Ministry Director. Known as "PBJ," volunteer pastor since 1997. Married 35+ years to Angela, 8 children. Passionate about helping men pursue God's best in relationships and faith.
- Jim Keen — Elder. Married to Roann since 1965 (as teenagers). 2 children, 5 grandchildren. Lifelong runner who climbed all 54 Colorado 14,000 ft peaks. Photographer and filmmaker for 40+ years with Christian ministries. Served in lay leadership roles.
- Howie Whitcomb — Elder. Married to Cari for 35+ years. 6 children (3 biological, 3 adopted) and 4 grandchildren. Grew up in California, moved to Colorado 20+ years ago. Heart for family ministry.

### Staff
- Joseph Tartaglia — Associate Pastor. Married high-school sweetheart Brooke in 1995. 7 children, 4 grandchildren. Enjoys listening and helping people find their next step with God. Loves strategy games, puzzles, zoo trips, and biking.
- Jonathan Darnell — Children and Youth Pastor. Joined MVC in Spring 2022 from New Mexico. Focus on discipleship and teaching youth to study Scripture and share faith boldly. Married to Faith, son Simeon.
- Matthew Smith — Worship Director. Deep love for music as a tool in worship. Coaches volleyball at Kiowa High School.
- Brenda Kelly — Office Manager and Financial Secretary. Joined MVC in 2005 with husband Les and 6 children. Served in AWANA, children's programs, and choir. Background in nonprofit administration and bookkeeping.
- Kristin Babcock — Ministry Assistant. Dedicated member since 2024. Married to Bodie. Enjoys camping, paddle-boarding, hiking, and snowboarding.
