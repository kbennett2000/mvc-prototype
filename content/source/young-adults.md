# Young Adults (18-25ish)

The ministry targets individuals 18–25 in transitional life stages — completed high school but not yet married. The group meets Monday evenings at 6:30 PM in "The Study" for Bible study with peer accountability and fellowship. The current curriculum focuses on the Gospel of Matthew, exploring how the apostle's account as a former tax collector presents Jesus as the promised Messiah. Refreshments are provided.
