# Subsplash

If your church uses the Subsplash platform, you can embed their giving page directly on your `/give` page using an iframe.

## What you need

- A Subsplash account with giving enabled.
- Your Subsplash giving page URL.

## Step 1 — Get your giving page URL

1. Log in to your Subsplash dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Giving** and find your giving page URL.
3. It typically looks like: `https://subsplash.com/churches/YOUR-CHURCH/give/app`

Contact Subsplash support if you're unsure of the correct embed URL.

## Step 2 — Configure in TinaCMS

1. Go to `/admin` → **Settings → Online Giving**.
2. Set **Giving Platform** to **Subsplash**.
3. Paste your giving page URL into **Subsplash: Giving Page URL**.
4. **Save**.

## How it works

The Subsplash giving page is embedded in a full-height iframe on your `/give` page.

## Verify

Visit `/give` on your site. The Subsplash giving form should appear embedded on the page. Test a small gift to confirm end-to-end.
