# Givelify

Givelify is popular in faith communities with a strong mobile giving experience and no monthly fees.

## What you need

- A Givelify organization account.
- Your Givelify **organization ID**.

## Step 1 — Find your organization ID

1. Log in to your Givelify dashboard at [givelify.com](https://www.givelify.com).
2. Go to **Settings → Organization Profile** or check the URL when viewing your public giving page.
3. Your giving page URL looks like: `https://www.givelify.com/give/XXXXX`
4. The `XXXXX` part is your organization ID.

## Step 2 — Configure in TinaCMS

1. Go to `/admin` → **Settings → Online Giving**.
2. Set **Giving Platform** to **Givelify**.
3. Enter your organization ID in **Givelify: Organization ID**.
4. **Save**.

## How it works

A "Give with Givelify" button appears on your `/give` page, linking to your Givelify giving page in a new tab.

## Verify

Visit `/give` and click the Givelify button. Confirm it opens your church's Givelify giving page.
