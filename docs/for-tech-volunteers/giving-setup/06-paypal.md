# PayPal

PayPal is the simplest option if your church doesn't yet have a dedicated giving platform. Most givers already have a PayPal account.

**Honest floor:** PayPal charges ~2.9% + 49¢ per transaction (or ~1.99% + 49¢ for registered nonprofits). Givers can also pay via credit card without a PayPal account. Consider a dedicated church platform once your giving volume justifies it.

## Step 1 — Get your donation URL

**Option A: PayPal.me (personal/business)**
- If you have a PayPal.me link (e.g. `https://www.paypal.me/YourChurchName`), you can use that directly.

**Option B: PayPal Nonprofit Donation Page (recommended)**
1. Go to [paypal.com/fundraiser](https://www.paypal.com/fundraiser) and create a giving page for your church.
2. Copy the URL of your fundraiser/donation page.

**Option C: PayPal Donate button URL**
1. In your PayPal Business account, go to **Tools → PayPal Buttons**.
2. Create a **Donate** button.
3. Get the direct URL from the button's hosted page.

## Step 2 — Configure in TinaCMS

1. Go to `/admin` → **Settings → Online Giving**.
2. Set **Giving Platform** to **PayPal**.
3. Paste your PayPal donation URL into **PayPal: Donation URL**.
4. **Save**.

## Verify

Visit `/give` and click **Donate via PayPal**. Confirm it opens your PayPal donation page.
