# Video: Add a staff member

**Length:** 2 minutes
**Audience:** Editors adding a new pastor, staff member, or office worker.
**Companion guide:** [04 Add a staff member](../for-editors/04-add-a-staff-member.md)

## Open with (10 sec)
**Say:** "In this video I'll show you how to add a new staff member to your church's site, including their photo and bio. It takes about two minutes."

## Steps (1 min 40 sec)

### 1. Open Staff (10 sec)
[Click **Staff** → **+ New Staff Member**.]

### 2. Fill in the form (1 min)
[Fill **Full Name**: type a name.]
**Say:** "Full name — how they want their name shown. Include 'Pastor' if it's part of how they're addressed."

[Fill **Role / Title**.]
**Say:** "Role is their job, like Senior Pastor or Office Manager."

[Fill **Email** OR leave blank.]
**Say:** "Email is optional. Leave it blank if they prefer to be contacted through the church office."

[Click the **Photo** field, browse to and select a photo file.]
**Say:** "Photo — square or close to square works best. The site crops it to a circle."

[Fill **Display Order** with a number.]
**Say:** "Display Order — `1` is first. Senior Pastor is usually 1, Associate Pastor 2, and so on."

[Type a bio in **Bio** field.]
**Say:** "Bio — two or three sentences. What someone meeting them on a Sunday would want to know."

### 3. Save and publish (20 sec)
[Click **Save** → **Status: Ready for Review** → **Publish**.]

## Wrap (10 sec)
[Show the new person on the About page or staff page.]
**Say:** "And they're added. After a volunteer approves, they appear on both the About page and the staff directory."
