# Video: Log in and tour the CMS

**Length:** 1.5 minutes
**Audience:** Brand-new editors logging in for the first time.
**Companion guide:** [01 Getting started](../for-editors/01-getting-started.md)

---

## Open with (10 sec)

[Browser open to a blank page.]

**Say:** "Hi — in this video I'll show you how to log in to your church's website editor and what you'll see on the dashboard. It takes about a minute and a half."

---

## Steps to record (1 min 10 sec)

### 1. Open the editor (15 sec)

[Type the church site URL, add `/admin/` at the end, press Enter.]

**Say:** "Open your church's site, then add `/admin/` at the end of the address. That takes you to the editor login page."

### 2. Sign in with GitHub (20 sec)

[Click **Login with GitHub**.]
[If prompted, sign in to GitHub with email and password.]
[On the authorization screen, click **Authorize**.]

**Say:** "Click Login with GitHub. Sign in if you're not already. The first time, GitHub will ask if you give permission for the site to use your account — click Authorize."

### 3. Tour the dashboard (25 sec)

[Hover over each collection in the left sidebar.]

**Say:** "On the left you'll see categories of things you can edit. Sermons. Ministries — the kids program, youth, women's, and so on. Staff. Elders. Site Settings is for church-wide info like address and phone. What We Believe is the doctrinal statements. Events is the calendar. And Pages is the Our Story content."

### 4. Show the workflow tab (10 sec)

[Click **Workflow** in the top right.]

**Say:** "Workflow at the top right shows changes you're still working on or that are waiting to go live."

---

## Wrap (10 sec)

[Show dashboard.]

**Say:** "That's the tour. The most common thing you'll do here is add a sermon — there's a separate video for that. Thanks for watching."

---

## On-screen text / captions

- 0:00 — Title card: "Logging in to your church website editor"
- 0:10 — Lower third showing URL: `yoursite.com/admin/`
- 0:35 — Caption: "Click Authorize the first time only"
- 0:55 — Labels for each collection as it's highlighted
- 1:25 — End card: "Next: Adding a sermon"

---

## Things to watch out for

- Don't record on a fresh account that has no draft history — viewers will be confused why their Workflow tab looks different.
- If the church's actual site is shown, blur or use a test account email.
